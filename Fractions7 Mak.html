1. Addition & Subtraction

Note: Answers can be positive or negative. Simplify all fractions.

10 Solved Examples

1. $\frac{1}{4} + \frac{2}{4} = \frac{3}{4}$
2. $\frac{5}{6} - \frac{1}{3} = \frac{5}{6} - \frac{2}{6} = \frac{1}{2}$
3. $-\frac{3}{8} + \frac{1}{8} = -\frac{2}{8} = -\frac{1}{4}$
4. $\frac{2}{5} - (-\frac{1}{5}) = \frac{3}{5}$
5. $1\frac{1}{2} + \frac{3}{4} = \frac{6}{4} + \frac{3}{4} = \frac{9}{4}$
6. $-\frac{2}{3} - \frac{1}{4} = -\frac{8}{12} - \frac{3}{12} = -\frac{11}{12}$
7. $\frac{7}{10} + (-\frac{2}{5}) = \frac{7}{10} - \frac{4}{10} = \frac{3}{10}$
8. $3 - \frac{4}{7} = \frac{21}{7} - \frac{4}{7} = \frac{17}{7}$
9. $-\frac{5}{12} - (-\frac{1}{6}) = -\frac{3}{12} = -\frac{1}{4}$
10. $\frac{1}{9} + \frac{1}{6} = \frac{2}{18} + \frac{3}{18} = \frac{5}{18}$

7. Decimal & Non-Decimal Fractions

Identify the Decimal Fraction (Denominator is a divisor of a power of 10)

5 Solved Examples

1. Between $\frac{5}{15}$ and $\frac{6}{30}$: **$\frac{6}{30}=\frac{1}{5}$**
2. Between $\frac{3}{30}$ and $\frac{5}{15}$: **$\frac{3}{30}=\frac{1}{10}$**
3. Between $\frac{15}{200}$ and $\frac{4}{60}$: **$\frac{15}{200}=\frac{3}{40}$**
4. Between $\frac{10}{200}$ and $\frac{15}{90}$: **$\frac{10}{200}=\frac{1}{20}$**
5. $0.45$ as a decimal fraction: **$\frac{45}{100}$**
