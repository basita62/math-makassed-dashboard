8. Algebraic Identities with Radicals

10 Solved Examples

1. $(\sqrt{3} + \sqrt{2})^2 = 3 + 2\sqrt{6} + 2 = 5 + 2\sqrt{6}$
2. $(\sqrt{5} - 1)^2 = 5 - 2\sqrt{5} + 1 = 6 - 2\sqrt{5}$
3. $(\sqrt{7} + \sqrt{3})(\sqrt{7} - \sqrt{3}) = 7 - 3 = 4$
4. $\sqrt{2}(3\sqrt{2} - 5) = 6 - 5\sqrt{2}$
5. $(\sqrt{2} + 3)(\sqrt{2} - 4) = 2 - 4\sqrt{2} + 3\sqrt{2} - 12 = -10 - \sqrt{2}$
6. $(2\sqrt{3} + 1)^2 = 12 + 4\sqrt{3} + 1 = 13 + 4\sqrt{3}$
7. $(\sqrt{10} - \sqrt{2})(\sqrt{10} + \sqrt{2}) = 10 - 2 = 8$
8. $3\sqrt{5}(\sqrt{5} + 2) = 15 + 6\sqrt{5}$
9. $(2 + \sqrt{3})(1 - 2\sqrt{3}) = 2 - 4\sqrt{3} + \sqrt{3} - 6 = -4 - 3\sqrt{3}$
10. $(2\sqrt{2} - 3\sqrt{3})^2 = 8 - 12\sqrt{6} + 27 = 35 - 12\sqrt{6}$
